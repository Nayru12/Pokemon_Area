Frequently Asked Questions

           
           

♦ How to acquire the cards I want?

           

→ You can consult the offers page to see the cards available for sale/exchange on our platform. Then you just need to contact the owner user to agree on the transaction. Another possibility is to register on the platform to proceed with the transaction, directly from our secure services ☺️

         

♦ Is the online payment secure?

           

→ Yes, we take care to ensure the security of the data used on our site, whether it is our users' data or our internal data. We comply with applicable standards, including those under the General Data Protection Regulation



♦ How long do I have to cancel a transaction (sale, purchase, exchange)?

           

→ The user with whom a customer makes a transaction has 24 hours to cancel the sale of their cards after making the transaction. A customer has 2 days to cancel a purchase. Both the user and the customer can cancel a card exchange within 7 days.



♦ What are the Rankings for?

→ The rankings show which users have the most cards, as well as which users have been rated the highest as a result of transactions.
